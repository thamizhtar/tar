# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 12, 2025

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

- **Personal Information:** We may collect information such as your name, email address, and other details you provide when using the app.
- **Usage Data:** We collect data about how you interact with the app to improve functionality and user experience.
- **Camera Access:** Our app may request access to your device's camera. Camera access is used solely for features that require capturing photos or scanning items within the app. We do not store or share your photos or camera data with third parties.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and improve app features and services
- To personalize your experience
- To ensure app security and integrity

## Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

## Camera Permissions

The app requests camera permissions only to enable specific features, such as capturing images or scanning barcodes. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or shared externally.
